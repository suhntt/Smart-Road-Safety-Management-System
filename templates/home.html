About Dibrugarh District Road Safety

The Dibrugarh District Road Safety Cell operates under the Government of Assam to ensure safer roads, reduce accidents, and promote traffic discipline and emergency preparedness.

Our Objectives

  • AI-based accident hotspot prediction
  • Integrated police and emergency response
  • Trauma care networks
  • GIS-based mapping and decision making
  • Road safety programs in schools and colleges
  • Public reporting of hazards and unsafe roads
